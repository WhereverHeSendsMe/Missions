South Asia Mission Trip

I am so excited to share this letter with you and express how the Lord has been moving in my life. I am currently a senior at the University of Central Oklahoma (UCO), and I am majoring in Graphic Design with a minor in illustration. With that being said, I am also heavily involved in my church Comunidad Cristiana Luz y Vida, where I serve on the worship team, translating team, and the kids' church. Freshman year of college I joined an organization on campus called the Baptist Collegiate Ministry (BCM). I have served on leadership there for four years and am currently the creative intern, and serve on the Freshmen Connection team as well as the worship and women's team! The BCM is an on campus ministry which strives to present the Gospel and who Jesus Christ is to students through intentional discipleship. Our goal is to provide community, and spiritual growth to our local campus. We are with you and for you. The BCM has helped me immensely in my walk with the Lord. Through discipleship, mentors, events, activities, trips, retreats etc, I have seen what it truly means to be on fire for the Lord, live radically for Jesus, and share the good news with the nations.

This summer I was presented with the incredible opportunity alongside the International Mission Board (IMB)to go with a team to South Asia to advance the gospel for about two weeks in Dec-Jan. I will be making several payments throughout the semester, and my goal is to have the full amount by the end of November.
