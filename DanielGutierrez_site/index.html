Hey friends!

After not going to church for about 6 years, in 2021, the Lord sent someone to invite me to church. Honestly, I didn't have any expectations for spiritual change. I was living in the world and thought I was happy. I was not aware of the brokenness of my own soul.

As some of you may know, the Lord has been opening doors in my life over this passed year that I wouldn't of even imagined.

He has allowed me to serve at FaithWorks.
He has allowed me to serve at Summit Camps.
He has allowed me to serve at Falls Creek.
He has allowed me to serve at Cartagena Colombia He has allowed me to serve His people every day.

Now, it looks like the Lord has provided another opportunity for me to serve the nations and spread His word. There will be a team of us going to Bujumbura and Gitega, Burundi Africa July 7th - 20th! We will be sharing the gospel through a youth camp, training local leaders, visiting churches, and so much more. In all we do His name will be glorified.

God is putting everything in place and working out the details.

I kept this section farely short to make an easy read. Please feel free to contact me if you'd like to know more! Look around this website; I plan on updating it periodically with photos, journal entries, missions, and FaithWorks!
